Lets throw it back to ancient times

The history of baking starts here in 600 BC were it is believed the ancient Greeks used to bake bread using using yeast, this eventually led to the creation of enclosed ovens. The world's oldest oven is currently believed to be around 6500 years old and was discovered in Croatia around 2014

2600 B.C.

Egyptians - Pioneers of Baking

Egyptians were at the forefront of baking which can be traced as far back as 2600 B.C. and are the reason we use yeast in bread today, although scholars debate the exact origin, as evidence suggests that Mesopotamians also produced yeast-risen bread. Egyptian bread was made almost exclusively from emmer wheat, which was more difficult to turn into flour than most other varieties of wheat.
